ABOUT US

CouncelX is a boutique Human Resources consulting firm providing a comprehensive range of HR solutions tailored to the needs of small businesses. Our core areas of expertise include HR audits and assessments, contract and vendor management support, immigration compliance, and policy formulation.

As a boutique firm, we are committed to delivering a highly personalized and collaborative approach. We engage closely with each client to understand their specific requirements and operational challenges, enabling us to offer cost-effective, targeted solutions that align with their organizational objectives.

OUR SERVICES

HR Audits and Assessments

Comprehensive evaluations of existing HR policies, practices, and systems to identify areas for improvement and establish strategic priorities.

Policy Development and Compliance

Assistance with the creation of customized HR policies, including employee handbooks, and guidance to ensure full compliance with applicable labor regulations.

Contract and Vendor Management

Support in managing HR-related vendor relationships and ensuring adherence to contractual obligations and internal policies.

Immigration Compliance

Advisory and support services to help ensure compliance with immigration laws and regulations relevant to employment practices.

Compliance Reviews and Corrective Action Planning

Conducting thorough compliance reviews and developing structured corrective action plans where necessary.

HR Business Partner Support

Strategic partnership to enhance the overall employee experience and support workforce planning, engagement, and retention.

Record-Keeping and Documentation

Assistance in establishing and maintaining compliant HR documentation and records.

Organizational Culture and Communication

Support in developing internal policies, communications strategies, and programs that strengthen organizational culture.

Remote Work Policy Development

Guidance on creating and implementing effective remote and hybrid work policies.
CouncelX operates on a retainer-based model, ensuring clients have consistent and reliable access to professional HR support as their business needs evolve.

We take pride in our ability to combine strategic insight with practical execution, offering each client a level of service and dedication that reflects the highest professional standards.
